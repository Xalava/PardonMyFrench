Pardon My French




Welcome to Pardon My French, a series of events dedicated to French Urban Culture, Entrepreneurship and Social Innovation.

Innovation is a culture

Startups, artists and non profits contribute to build the world of tomorrow. At Pardon My French, we believe that cultural exchanges between communities from different fields and countries can enchance innovation, creativity and social impact.

What do we do?

We organize kick-ass cultural events. With digital art, live performance and showcasing, they are designed to be a fun moment of high quality content & exchanges.

How can I participate?

Our events are free, and open to the general public, join us!

if you have a project or skills you would like to share, just shoot us an email at whatsup@pardonmyfrench.io
